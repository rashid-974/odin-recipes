Chocolate Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 180g Dark Chocolate
  • 440g Cream Cheese
  • 120g Sugar
  • 10g Cocoa Powder
  • 1tsp Vanilla
  • 3 Eggs
  • 240g Whipping Cream

Steps

  1. Get some baking sheet and place it in the baking tin.
  2. Melt the chocolate in a bowl using the microwave.
  3. Add the cheese and sugar to a bowl and mix it till thoroughly incorporated.
  4. Add cocoa powder and vanilla essence to it and combine.
  5. Add the 3 eggs one by one to the mixture and combine.
  6. Add the whipping cream and mix till combined
  7. Strain the mixture using a sieve into the mold to remove the lumpy bits.
  8. Bake it in a preheated oven at 220°C for 30-35 minutes.
  9. After taking it out the oven, let it come to room temperature and refrigerate it overnight or at least for 6 hours before serving.
